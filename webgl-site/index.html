About

A digital artist exploring the intersection of technology, light, and perception. Crafting immersive visual experiences through code and creativity.

Each piece is a meditation on depth, infinity, and the beauty of algorithmic design.

Works

Orbital Depth — An exploration of concentric symmetry and infinite depth.

Philosophy

Simplicity through complexity. Every line, every curve, every gradient serves a purpose.

Technology is a canvas. Code is poetry. Pixels tell stories.

Design should breathe. Animation should feel natural. Interaction should be intuitive.

Experiments

Pushing the boundaries of WebGL and Three.js.

Real-time shader compilation. GPU-accelerated particle systems. Procedural geometry.

Always exploring. Always iterating. Always learning.
